Expected distance from disk center

December 10, 2025 · 1 min
Question
Choose a point uniformly at random from the unit disk. What is the expected distance from the point to the center?
Solution

Let $R$ denote the distance from the center. For $0 \le r \le 1$, $$ \mathbb{P}[R \le r] = \frac{\pi r^2}{\pi \cdot 1^2} = r^2. $$

Thus the density is $$ f_R(r) = 2r. $$

Therefore, $$ \mathbb{E}[R] = \int_0^1 r \cdot 2r, dr = \frac{2}{3}. $$
